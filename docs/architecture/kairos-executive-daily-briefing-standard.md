# Kairos Executive Daily Briefing Standard

Status: Approved architecture.

Purpose:
Standardize every Morning Check-In and Evening Wrap-Up.

Morning briefing order:
1. Greeting
2. Overall Health Check
3. Executive Summary
4. Overnight accomplishments
5. Top priorities
6. Decisions requiring attention
7. Recommended first action
8. Six-button navigation

Evening wrap-up order:
1. Executive summary
2. Accomplishments
3. Business metrics and trajectory
4. Completed deliveries and exports
5. Risks and blockers
6. Proposed overnight plan
7. Executive approval

Rule:
Every briefing should be readable in under five minutes while allowing drill-down into every section.
