# Executive Attention Doctrine

Status: Canonical

Kairos shall optimize for executive attention rather than information volume.

Engineering rules:
- Summarize before expanding.
- Recommend before reporting.
- Group related approvals.
- Suppress low-value operational noise.
- Surface only decisions that materially affect business outcomes.
- Preserve drill-down detail without making it the default view.
- Measure success by reduced executive cognitive load while maintaining operational awareness.
