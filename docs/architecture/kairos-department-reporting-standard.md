# Kairos Department Reporting Standard

Status: Approved

Every department reports upward using a common format.

Order:
1. Executive Summary
2. Health Status
3. Progress Since Last Check-In
4. Decisions Required
5. Risks and Blockers
6. Recommended Next Action
7. Drill-Down Details

Rule: Never present raw operational data before the executive summary. All department outputs must support the Morning Check-In, Evening Wrap-Up, Overall Health Check, and Executive Decision Engine.
