# Kairos Executive Exception Management Standard

## Status
Approved architecture.

## Purpose
Ensure the executive is interrupted only when intervention materially improves outcomes.

## Exception Levels
- Critical: Immediate executive attention.
- High: Action required before dependent work proceeds.
- Medium: Include in next Morning Check-In or Evening Wrap-Up.
- Low: Log and monitor without interrupting the executive.

## Routing Rules
- Resolve automatically whenever approved policies permit.
- Escalate only when approvals, strategic direction, legal, financial, security, or brand decisions are required.
- Bundle related exceptions into a single executive package.
- Suppress duplicate notifications.
- Record every exception, resolution, and escalation path.

## Success Metric
Executives spend time resolving high-value exceptions instead of reacting to routine operational noise.
