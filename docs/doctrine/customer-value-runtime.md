# Customer Value Runtime Doctrine

## Status

Locked operating doctrine for Mindset Media Group and Kairos runtime surfaces.

## Canonical Promise

**Your Knowledge Has Value.**

Supporting statement:

**Helping you discover it, build it, and share it with the world.**

## Strategic Positioning

Mindset Media Group is not positioned primarily as a publishing company, AI company, or creator tool company.

MMG is positioned as an integrated system that helps people uncover, organize, package, preserve, and share the value already contained in their knowledge, experience, creativity, skills, and perspective.

The customer-facing emphasis is not get-rich language or shortcut income claims. The emphasis is guided capability-building, durable asset creation, and long-term opportunity.

## Customer Journey Sequence

Future customer-facing copy and product surfaces should follow this sequence when practical:

1. **Outcome** — Help the customer move toward income, opportunity, independence, or growth.
2. **Identity** — Reinforce that the customer already has valuable knowledge, experience, creativity, or perspective.
3. **Agency** — Show that they can build something real from that value.
4. **Guidance** — Position MMG and Kairos as steady teachers, guides, and operating partners.
5. **System** — Present the ecosystem as the connected operating system that helps package, market, monetize, and improve what the customer uniquely offers.

## Kairos Runtime Role

Kairos is the intelligence layer that keeps the customer moving forward.

Kairos should:

- preserve context,
- organize projects,
- recommend next actions,
- connect isolated ideas into a body of work,
- help transform content into assets,
- maintain consistency across channels,
- and support disciplined execution without generic hype.

Kairos should not be presented merely as an AI assistant. It is the guide, memory, strategist, and operating layer inside the MMG ecosystem.

## Knowledge Stewardship Rule

Treat customer work as a long-term body of work rather than isolated content.

Ideas, posts, books, videos, products, services, lessons, notes, workflows, and campaigns should be preserved and connected so they compound into durable knowledge assets over time.

## Approved Voice

Use language centered on:

- guidance,
- education,
- stewardship,
- practical execution,
- patience,
- integrity,
- long-term value,
- confidence,
- capability,
- and customer growth.

Avoid language centered on:

- easy money,
- get-rich schemes,
- hype,
- passive income shortcuts,
- unrealistic guarantees,
- or manipulative scarcity.

## Implementation Requirement

Website pages, dashboard modules, Shopify surfaces, customer onboarding, product copy, service descriptions, and Kairos runtime cards should reinforce this doctrine wherever customer-facing positioning is involved.

The doctrine should be treated as a durable brand standard unless explicitly amended by executive direction.
