# MMG Digital Asset Edition V2 Customer Release Doctrine

## Status

Permanent MMG/Kairos publishing and production doctrine.

## Canonical Template

The approved **AI Video Prompt Mastery — MMG Digital Asset Edition V2 Production Master** is the canonical manuscript-writing, editorial, formatting, and production template for future MMG digital assets.

The subject changes by title. The manufacturing standard does not.

## Manuscript Standard

Every MMG digital asset must be written and rebuilt as a substantial customer operating manual or field guide rather than a thin ebook.

A compliant manuscript must:

- contain at least 100 substantive finished pages;
- use a clear part-and-chapter progression;
- convert knowledge into reusable systems;
- include practical architecture appropriate to the subject, such as frameworks, workflows, prompt blocks, templates, worksheets, checklists, decision rules, implementation tools, labs, creator notes, pro tips, and action steps;
- use deliberate chapter openings, page breaks, typography, hierarchy, callouts, front matter, and back matter;
- avoid filler, duplicated passages, artificial spacing, and weak pagination;
- be rewritten and substantively expanded when it does not meet the standard;
- use the Master Production DOCX as the canonical source of truth for downstream generation.

No downstream customer PDF may be edited independently of the Master Production DOCX. Revisions originate in the master and all affected deliverables are regenerated.

## Customer-Facing Rule

Every item in the customer release package must be written for the customer.

This includes the PDF specification sheet.

Customer files must not contain:

- internal production instructions;
- private links or URLs;
- workflow commentary;
- QA reports or release manifests;
- Admin Asset Vault language;
- storefront implementation instructions;
- Shopify content or metadata;
- unresolved notes, placeholders, or production commentary;
- individual author attribution.

## Identity Rule

**Michael King must not appear anywhere in the production package or customer release.**

The restriction includes visible text, filenames, covers, title pages, copyright pages, metadata, embedded author fields, headers, footers, acknowledgments, back matter, manifests, package records, image alt text, and document properties.

The only publisher and creator identity is:

**Mindset Media Group™**

## Exact Customer Release Package

The controlling customer release contains exactly six deliverables:

1. Customer-facing PDF specification sheet
2. KDP Interior PDF, 6 × 9 inches, without an embedded front cover
3. Customer-ready Digital Asset Edition V2 PDF with the approved cover as page one
4. Portrait cover image, PNG, 2048 × 3072 pixels
5. Square thumbnail cover image, PNG, 2048 × 2048 pixels, with the full portrait cover visible
6. Customer-facing README.txt

The complete customer ZIP must contain only those six deliverables.

## Internal Production Artifact

The Master Production DOCX remains mandatory inside the governed production system and Admin Asset Vault. It is not automatically included in the customer-facing ZIP.

Other internal records—including QA reports, research records, production manifests, storefront files, and product metadata—remain internal unless a separate approved release contract explicitly includes them.

## Cover Rules

- The portrait cover uses the canonical 2:3 presentation at 2048 × 3072 pixels.
- The square thumbnail uses 2048 × 2048 pixels.
- The full portrait cover must remain visible.
- Cropping is prohibited.
- Redrawing is prohibited.
- The square derivative uses intentional padding or background accommodation.
- The KDP Interior remains cover-free.
- The Digital Edition uses the approved cover as page one.

## Release Gate

Kairos must fail the release when any of the following is true:

- the manuscript is below 100 substantive finished pages;
- the practical operating-manual architecture is incomplete;
- excessive repeated content or padding is detected;
- an individual attribution appears;
- internal or Shopify content appears in a customer deliverable;
- the customer ZIP contains more or fewer than six deliverables;
- the portrait or thumbnail dimensions are incorrect;
- a cover derivative crops or redraws the approved cover;
- the KDP Interior includes the front cover;
- the Digital Edition does not include the approved cover as page one.
