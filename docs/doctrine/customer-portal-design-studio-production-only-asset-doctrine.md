# Customer Portal Design Studio — Production-Only Asset Doctrine

## Status

Permanent MMG/Kairos implementation doctrine.

## Governing Rule

The Customer Portal Design Studio is a production workspace inside the MMG/Kairos customer portal. It is not a free standalone creative platform, generic design tool, or external asset-generation utility.

Any asset created, generated, edited, assembled, refined, or managed inside the Customer Portal Design Studio is intended to remain inside the MMG/Kairos ecosystem unless explicitly released as an approved final deliverable under the applicable product, service, package, subscription, licensing tier, or administrative approval.

## Purpose

The Design Studio exists to support MMG customer production workflows, including:

- Publishing preparation
- Editorial production
- Formatting
- Cover design
- Brand asset development
- Marketing asset development
- Digital asset production
- Print preparation
- Customer project preparation
- Production review and approval workflows
- Other approved MMG production workflows

The Design Studio should give customers enough creative capability to prepare and refine their work inside the platform while keeping that work connected to MMG’s production, fulfillment, publishing, and delivery pipeline.

## In-House Asset Retention

By default, the following Design Studio materials stay in-house inside the customer’s MMG/Kairos workspace:

- Intermediate assets
- Editable files
- Layered files
- Drafts
- AI generations
- Generation history
- Templates
- Project files
- Reusable components
- Source files
- Production materials
- Internal layout systems
- Preview assets
- Work-in-progress creative files

These materials are platform workspace assets, not unrestricted exports.

## Export and Release Control

Customers receive the approved final deliverables tied to what they purchased or are licensed to receive.

Any export, download, reproduction, external-use permission, source-file transfer, or asset release must be explicitly authorized by one or more of the following:

- Product rules
- Service terms
- Licensing tier
- Subscription entitlement
- Customer purchase
- Project status
- Administrative approval
- Defined delivery workflow

The default position is no unrestricted export of editable, intermediate, reusable, or source production assets.

## Anti-Bypass Principle

The Design Studio must not become a free design platform that allows users to generate, design, reproduce, download, or externally use assets created in MMG/Kairos to bypass MMG services.

Design Studio tools should strengthen the MMG ecosystem by moving customer-created work into MMG’s production, approval, publishing, fulfillment, and delivery workflows.

## Enforcement Requirements

Kairos and the Customer Portal should enforce this doctrine through platform controls, including where appropriate:

- Permission gates
- Licensing checks
- Subscription entitlement checks
- Project status controls
- Approval gates
- Workspace restrictions
- Export restrictions
- Watermarking or preview-only states
- Audit history
- Asset provenance tracking
- Administrative release controls
- Clear customer-facing terms

## Engineering Doctrine

Future portal, backend, iOS, dashboard, licensing, customer agreement, production workflow, and engineering decisions must preserve this production-only asset doctrine.

Design Studio capabilities should be modular, customer-useful, and production-connected, but they must remain aligned with MMG’s in-house production pipeline and business model.
