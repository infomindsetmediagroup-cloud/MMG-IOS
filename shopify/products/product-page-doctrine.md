# MMG Product Page Doctrine

## Standard Single-Variant Product Pages

Canonical base: MMG-AI-PROMPTING-FOR-BEGINNERS-v1.3.1-CAROUSEL-SERVICE-PRICE-FIX.

Applies to regular MMG products and digital products unless the user explicitly directs otherwise.

## Multi-Variant Product Pages

Canonical base: MMG-PUBLISH-READY-BOOK-BUILD-SERVICE-v1.9-EDITORIAL-PREMIUM-UX.

Applies to any MMG catalog product with multiple Shopify variants, regardless of category.

## Multi-Variant Requirements

Each live variant card must show:

1. Variant or package name
2. Price alone
3. Variant-specific scope, count, or descriptor alone
4. Description and details
5. Variant-specific purchase action

## Carousel Requirements

Use the autonomous MMG Intelligent Carousel Engine v2.0 by default:

- no visible manual arrows
- hands-free continuous auto-loop
- smooth one-card advancement
- swipe or drag support
- automatic resume after interaction
- infinite looping
- Shopify catalog-driven product loading
- current product exclusion
- intelligent recommendation ordering
- full image containment

## Preservation Rule

When rebuilding a product page from existing code, preserve product-specific content, intent, metadata, links, price, handle, imagery behavior, and customer-facing copy unless deliberately changed.
