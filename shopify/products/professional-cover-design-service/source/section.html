How It Works

The MMG Production Engine™ moves the project through a clear workflow.

  1. Purchase the correct package.Shopify confirms the order and delivers the Project Guide.
  2. Complete Customer Portal intake.Submit the project objective, references, and files.
  3. Validate scope and source files.MMG confirms the work matches the purchased tier.
  4. Move the cover into production.Composition, typography, hierarchy, and presentation are developed.
  5. Review and quality assure.Readability, consistency, file quality, and completeness are checked.
  6. Receive the delivery package.Final assets and instructions are organized and released.
