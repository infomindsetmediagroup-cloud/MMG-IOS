# Kairos Command Center Registry

## Foundational Vertical Slices

| Command Center | Status | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Admin Operations Center | Foundational | Internal control plane for MMG operations. |
| Customer Portal | Foundational | Customer-facing hub for onboarding, orders, project updates, files, and service lifecycle. |
| Professional Publishing Command Center | Foundational | Publishing operating system layer for books, products, knowledge assets, editorial work, and production records. |
| Production Command Center | Foundational | Manufacturing and execution layer for pages, products, service deliverables, and assets. |
| Quality and Release Center | Foundational | QA, validation, release readiness, changelogs, and deployment tracking. |
| Automation and Intelligence Center | Next major slice | Automation, recommendations, intelligence workflows, and operator acceleration. |
| Growth, Marketing and Advertising Command Center | Foundational | Campaigns, promotions, acquisition, retention, ads, email, segmentation, and lifecycle marketing. |

## Operating Direction

Kairos should continue as an enterprise product with complete end-to-end operational features. New work should measurably increase production readiness and platform continuity.

## Human Approval Rule

External marketing or advertising campaign launch requires human approval unless explicitly changed.
