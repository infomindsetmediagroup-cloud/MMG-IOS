# MMG Brand Baseline

## Brand

Mindset Media Group is the parent company and publisher. Kairos is the flagship operating platform.

## Positioning

Books. AI. Business. Creator Education.

Practical knowledge for modern creators.

## Visual Language

- Premium, modern, professional
- Apple-style spacing and rhythm
- Blue accent system
- High contrast typography
- Clean page architecture
- No unnecessary clutter
- Strong editorial confidence

## Logo Notes

The circular metallic MMG logo is the preferred permanent default brand asset when available.

## Common CTA Language

- Follow the journey.
- Build with purpose.
- Post. Learn. Improve. Build in public.
- Choose your next step.
- Turn knowledge into assets.

## Founder Context

Founder: Michael King.

Core story pattern: from professional Honda auto technician to digital creator and publisher, building MMG as a practical knowledge ecosystem and legacy-driven company.
